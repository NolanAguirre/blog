Inline Spinner Recommendation

Fishing February 10, 2026

The Bass Pro Shops XPS Lazer Eye Micro Spin in natural shad has been working well for me.

I've been using it in shallow creek beds with clear, fast-flowing water. It's caught both smallmouth and largemouth bass.

Where I know there are bass, I've had success with it.

One concern: the spoon doesn't always spin well. Out of several lures, I've had issues with two where the spoon doesn't spin properly, maybe 20% of the time. I just buy multiple lures at a time.
